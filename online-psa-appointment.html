PSA Online Appointment (2025): Complete Step-by-Step Guide

Need a PSA birth, marriage, death certificate, or CENOMAR? This static page contains the essential steps to book, what to prepare, and how to avoid delays. For the full site experience and printable checklists, visit onlinepsaappointment.com.

Quick steps

  1. Choose document type (birth, marriage, death, CENOMAR).
  2. Prepare the exact names, birthdates, and IDs required.
  3. Book online or choose a PSA outlet for walk-in/claim.
  4. Pay via supported channels and keep your reference number.
  5. Track the delivery or return on the pickup date.

Common FAQs

  • Do I need an appointment? — Some outlets require one; others allow limited walk-ins.
  • Can I request for someone else? — Yes. Bring authorization letter and IDs. See sample Authorization Letter for PSA.
  • How long is delivery? — Varies by location and courier; keep your phone reachable.
